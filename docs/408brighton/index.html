What You'll Get

The coastal colors of outside are captured inside this spacious 3-bed, 3-bath ocean view condo that offers all the comforts of home. Situated on the 4th floor of beachfront Brighton Towers, this newly renovated property offers beautifully appointed decor. Relax on one of the balconies off the master, living room, or 3rd bedroom. Overlook the spectacular white sandy beach right outside your door. Can sleep up to 10 people with two king-sized beds, two double beds, and a pull-out sofa in the family room. Kingston Plantation offers a myriad of amenities and the best of both worlds...it's minutes from all the action and hundreds of golf courses and attractions; yet, it's on a private beach, with pools, pristine grounds and tranquil settings.

Full of Amenities

Enjoy all the luxurious amenities Kingston Plantation has to offer, including heated outdoor and indoor pools; nearby golf courses; Jacuzzi's; tennis, volleyball and pickle ball courts; playground; miles of walking and nature trails surrounding the resort; many beach accesses with a boardwalk along the beach; water sports; bike and golf cart rentals; Apache Pier fishing; restaurants and bars; 78 Fitness and Health Club at the Landing; and Spa33. A 50,000 sq. ft. resort water park is located beside Brighton offering a lazy river, three whirlpools, and a Caribbean Water Playground, complete with water slides, jet sprays and more. The Hilton Resort has a sand bottom pool. Other resort features include:

  • Private parking deck adjoined to Brighton
  • Resort shuttle service, which includes areas within a one-mile loop of the resort
  • Cashless charging privileges on the resort
  • 24-hour on-site maintenance and engineering staff
  • Daily housekeeping services
  • Entrance security gate and 24-hour resort security
  • Resort Wi-Fi available
  • Rental of poolside cabanas at Hilton Myrtle Beach Resort pool deck
